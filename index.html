SouthPoint School & College is an English medium school with an aim to provide students with high quality education through English medium.The teachers of SouthPoint School & College are well trained to teach good quality of education through the medium of English.Subjects like Digital Technology, Chemistry,Physical Education, taught practically here.

Welcome to SPSC education school

SPSC is an English Medium School & College with clear difference in both syllabuses and approaches to teaching. From Play Group to Class V, we follow the common syllabuses with other English Medium schools in the country. However, from class VI onward we switch over to our mainstream education and prepare our students for the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations in the medium of English following the Board (NCTB), Bangladesh.
